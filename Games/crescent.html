Crescent Revamped

❮ ❯ 

    Crescent is a 2D side-scrolling RPG and puzzle solving game made in Unity. 
  Alexander Dispenza(my friend) and I worked on this project during our 2nd year of 
  Game Programming. 
    
  The lore behind the game consists of the main protagonist(Lua) and his sidekick(Crescent).
  Together they are Crescent Lua, which translates to Crescent Moon. (Lua is a programming language that also means Moon in Portuguese).
  Their objective throughout the game is to fight off evil with the assistance of Lua's Grandfather. Crescent is also very useful to 
  Lua when it comes to the puzzle solving aspect. 
    
  As for the game itself, we were able to implement a handful of game mechanics, a tutorial level, and two regular levels. 
  It was definitely a very fun experience and I will hopefully revisit this project eventually.
